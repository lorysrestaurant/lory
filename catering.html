Catering

Catering must be for Parties of 15 people or more.

Please call in your order at least one day in advance.

All orders come in party trays, meaning that all orders must be placed by telling us your per person count.

For catering, your party is not only limited to our menu, order can be custom creations.

We offer hot serving chafing dishes and waiters.

For parties of 14 or less, please pick up from the restaurant, as a take-out oder Monday through Friday from 11:00am to 3:00pm.
